# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 26, 2025**

At Splitly, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Who Collects Your Data?

Splitly is operated by an independent developer. Contact: qqwang2025@gmail.com 

## What Data Do We Collect and Why?

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Name** | To identify you within expense groups and display to other group members |
| **Email Address** | To identify your account, enable login, and send important notifications |
| **Profile Picture** | To visually enhance the app and distinguish users within groups |
| **Expense Data** | Groups, transactions, members, and history - for the core functionality of the app |
| **Authentication Data** | OAuth tokens from third-party providers (Google, Apple) for secure login |

We do **not** collect:
- Your location data
- Your contacts
- Your financial/banking information (we only track expense records you enter)

## Where Is Your Data Stored?

All data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure, which uses industry-standard security practices. Data may be stored in data centers located in the United States or Europe.

## How Is Your Data Secured?

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are never stored in plain text
- We use OAuth for third-party authentication (Google Sign-In)
- Our cloud provider (Supabase) employs enterprise-grade security measures

## What Is Shared With Other Users?

Other members in your expense groups can see:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your profile picture
- Expenses and transactions you create within the group

## What Is Shared With Third Parties?

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

| Third Party | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| **Supabase** | Cloud database and authentication services |
| **Google** | Authentication (when using Google Sign-In) |
| **Apple** | Authentication (when using Sign in with Apple) |

We may also share data when required by law or necessary to comply with valid legal processes.

## How Long Is Your Data Stored?

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

## How to Delete Your Data?

You can delete your account and all associated data by:
1. Going to the Profile section in the app
2. Selecting "Delete Account"
3. Confirming the deletion

Alternatively, contact us at **qqwang2025@gmail.com** to request data deletion.



## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
